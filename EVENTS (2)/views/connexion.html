CONNEXION

The IoT Applications Club launched its first-ever IoT Hackathon, "Connexion", inviting enthusiastic freshmen to explore the vast world of Internet of Things through innovation and teamwork.

About the Event

CONNEXION is IOTA's flagship hackathon designed exclusively for freshmen to dive into the exciting world of IoT. This event provides a platform for budding technologists to connect, collaborate, and create innovative IoT solutions to real-world problems.

Event Highlights

  • 24-hour hackathon focused on IoT solutions
  • Beginner-friendly workshops and mentorship
  • Hands-on experience with IoT hardware and software
  • Networking with industry professionals
  • Exciting prizes and goodies for winners

Event Details

Date: Coming Soon

Time: 24-hour event (overnight)

Venue: Jadavpur University, Kolkata

Team Size: 2-4 members

Eligibility: Open to all first-year students

What You'll Learn

  • Basics of IoT and its applications
  • Working with microcontrollers and sensors
  • Data collection and analysis
  • Prototyping and presentation skills
