About the Event

INNOVATIA 2025 is the flagship event of IOTA (IoT Applications Club), bringing together innovators, tech enthusiasts, and industry experts for a celebration of technology and creativity. This year's theme focuses on "Sustainable Innovation for a Better Tomorrow."

Event Highlights

  • Cutting-edge workshops on IoT, AI, and emerging technologies
  • Hackathon with exciting problem statements
  • Tech exhibitions showcasing student projects
  • Panel discussions with industry leaders
  • Networking opportunities with like-minded individuals
